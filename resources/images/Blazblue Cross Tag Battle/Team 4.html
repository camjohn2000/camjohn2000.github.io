Team 4

Neo Politan

For 2.0, I wanted to play 80% of the new characters. Man.....she was supposed to be a part of my secondary team, but her teammate is/was unviable online so...I did not originally play her, but then I really gave her a shot. Now, she is my gal! I play her over anyone. She has hella short comings but she has MAJOR utility and potential! She is the Air-to-Air Gaaaaawwwwdddd!!!!

Tohru Adachi

His personality blew me away. He seemed like the character that would piss you off getting hit by. His laughing, taunting, cursing, demeanor, boooiiiii!!!! He hits hella hard and has a great Persona! He has his shortcomings and has bad midrange, but he can play up close and far away. Dope character and fun with hella options!
